Coronavirus Rules

Due to the recent change in restrictions, some considerations will need to be made so that we comply with current social distancing regulations.

  • If yourself, or someone you have had close contact with recently, have any coronavirus symptoms please do NOT attend and keep us informed.
  • Please wear masks whilst indoors and not sat down eating/drinking.
  • Be respectful and keep a distance of 2 metres with other guests and staff not in your household/bubble.
  • You will be able to move between tables after the meal, but please only eat/drink while sat down.
  • Food and drink will be table service.
